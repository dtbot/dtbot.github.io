Introducing

Doubting Tom

Your tool for doubt resolution





Doubting Tom makes learning by yourself, and helping others learn, a fun and rewarding activity on discord. It includes a comprehensive suite of functions to tabulate queries, collect and display answers, and reward those who answer.

Features

Ask a Question

dt.doubt - Other server members can now answer the question using Tom. The bot replies to a dt.doubt command with an identification number, which is used to refer to the query while answering it.

Answer a Question

dt.answer - This offers your explanation as a solution to a specific question. The answer is offered to the user who asked the question, to verify its veracity. If verified, 5 points will be added to your score.

Collect the Answers

dt.getanswer - All the verified explanations are sent to you as direct messages.
